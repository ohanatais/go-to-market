# Mode: Guided

For a user getting their first users from zero, for the first time, who wants to understand the
approach — not just a list of tactics. Walk the five layers in order, explaining the why, so they
can keep doing it as 10 becomes 50 becomes 100.

Load `references/core-principles.md` and `references/components.md` alongside this file.

## Posture

Teaching, and a little reassuring — getting first users feels daunting and the manual approach
feels "too small" until you explain *why* it's the actual strategy. Lead with the reframe (found,
not attracted; 10, not a million; do things that don't scale). Anchor everything in *their*
specific users. Keep pulling vague answers back to named people and places.

## The flow

**Step 0 — readiness check.** Is there a usable product and a sense of who has the problem? If
they're really pre-build testing an idea, redirect to `/personas` / idea validation. If the
target is vague, the first work is getting specific.

**Step 1 — Reframe.** Set the target: 10 true users, found by hand, in a short time box. Name
what they're *not* doing yet (viral, ads). This unlocks the rest.

**Step 2 — Find them.** Turn the persona into places and recent signals; build a concrete list of
reachable, specific people who have the problem now.

**Step 3 — The conversation.** Teach the Mom Test. Draft an outreach opener and the questions
that learn rather than pitch, in their language. Stress: the offer comes last and small.

**Step 4 — Onboard by hand.** Plan the unscalable, overwhelmingly-good first experience for the
people who say yes — personal setup, over-delivery, close attention to the first session.

**Step 5 — Turn into engine.** Set up the stay-vs-churn watch, the "ask why they left," and the
one-specific-introduction ask. Show how the first cohort feeds the next.

## Checkpoints

After each layer, reflect it back and confirm. The make-or-break is Step 2 — if the "find list"
is still a demographic and not named people/places, don't move on; outreach into vagueness fails.

## Close

Deliver the full **Output always includes** set from SKILL.md. Hand off to `/product-copy` for
the messages and landing page, and to `/launch-strategy` once a repeatable channel emerges from
the manual work. Leave them with the truth: this is slow, manual, unglamorous work — and it's the
work. Ten who stay and one who refers beats a thousand who bounce.
